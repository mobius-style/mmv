## Mandatory framing paragraph (suggested wording, verbatim)

> Phase 5C measurement on MMV Core v0.1-rc1 (the post-freeze
> baseline) surfaced a bimodal pattern: governance wins on
> restraint-needed categories, loses on direct-answer categories.
> A two-line route-calibration patch (v0.1-rc2 candidate, commit
> `daf2a8c`) targets the direct-answer loss. Re-judging the same
> 60-pair Phase 5C set with the same Groq `openai/gpt-oss-120b`
> judge shows the bimodal split persists but is flattened: overall
> Δ (governed − raw) moved from −0.44 to +0.24, and direct-answer
> categories tightened by 1–3 points each in score. We do not
> claim deployment-wide effect — the same 60 pairs were used to
> diagnose and to repair-check, and a fresh-holdout judge run is the
> right next step before promotion.

## Required caveat block (verbatim)

> **Caveats.** The rc2 repair-check uses the same 60 pairs that
> surfaced the rc1 weakness; this is **not** an independent
> validation. Raw responses were re-used verbatim; only the
> governed side was regenerated by the rc2 runtime. Single judge
> (Groq `openai/gpt-oss-120b`); the rc1 and rc2 judge runs use the
> same judge and rubric. Length-bias guard explicitly included in
> the judge prompt. Pseudo-UI only; UI-layer surface remains
> untested. NOT Phase 6 full evaluation. NOT deployment-wide
> validation.

## What the paper SHOULD claim

- "RC2 reduced the over-verification penalty on direct-answer
  prompts. The conceptual_explanation cell improved by +3.12 score
  points; specialized_terminology by +1.33."
- "The bimodal split persists in rc2 but is flattened. Restraint-
  needed categories were preserved (ambiguous_underspecified
  actually gained +1.11)."
- "The repair is a two-line calibration patch, not a redesign. The
  Phase 5C measurement (rc1) remains the load-bearing observation;
  rc2 is the iteration."

## What the paper should NOT claim

- "MMV is now better than raw at direct-answer." It is not — rc2
  still loses on direct-answer cells; the loss is smaller.
- "Statistically significant improvement at n=54." Not at this
  sample size.
- "RC2 is the production version." It is a candidate. Promotion to
  public-release is a separate operator decision.

## Suggested narrative arc for the paper

1. **Phase 4 closure** establishes Pattern Library / governance /
   Cat-A surface.
2. **Phase 5A freeze** locks v0.1-rc1.
3. **Phase 5C / P9 Evidence Pack v1** measures rc1 with pseudo-UI
   evidence pack: bimodal split observed.
4. **Normalization wiring audit** identifies two narrow causes
   inside the freeze policy's allowed-class envelope.
5. **Phase 5D rc2 candidate** applies a two-line calibration
   targeting those causes.
6. **This repair-check (P9-RC2)** measures the rc2 candidate on the
   same 60 pairs: bimodal split flattened; direct-answer categories
   tighter; restraint-needed categories preserved.
7. **Future**: fresh holdout + non-`openai/gpt-oss` judge, decide
   whether to promote rc2 to public-release tag.

The paper's load-bearing observation is **the bimodal split**, not
the absolute mean. RC2 strengthens the framing by showing that the
split is **calibration-tunable** with surgical-scope changes, not a
fundamental tradeoff baked into the architecture.
